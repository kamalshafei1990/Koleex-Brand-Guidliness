Background Style

The official system for backgrounds, surfaces, and visual environments used across all Koleex brand touchpoints. Every background must reinforce the industrial, premium, and technological character of the brand.

Background System

Backgrounds Are Fundamental to Brand Identity

Backgrounds define the visual environment in which every Koleex communication exists. They are not empty space — they are engineered surfaces that set the tone, mood, and hierarchy of every design. A correct background reinforces brand authority; an incorrect one destroys it instantly.

Minimal Industrial Technological Premium Monochrome Structural Controlled Precise
Background Principles

Ten core principles that govern every background decision in the Koleex brand system. These principles ensure consistency, readability, and brand integrity.

01
Backgrounds must always be monochrome — black, white, and gray only. No color backgrounds are permitted.
02
Every background must provide sufficient contrast for text and logo readability (minimum 4.5:1 ratio).
03
Gradients are permitted only in grayscale — from dark to darker, or light to lighter. Never cross the midpoint.
04
Textures must be subtle and industrial — brushed metal, concrete, or technical grid. Never organic or decorative.
05
Glass and blur effects are allowed only as overlays on approved base backgrounds.
06
Backgrounds must never compete with content — they support and frame, never distract.
07
Pattern backgrounds must use geometric, grid-based designs only. No organic patterns.
08
The logo must always remain clearly visible on any background — test contrast before use.
09
Dark backgrounds are preferred for premium, technical, and hero contexts. Light backgrounds for content-heavy layouts.
10
Every background choice must be intentional — never use a background by default without considering the context.

Do & Don't

Quick reference for correct and incorrect background usage across all Koleex brand communications.

Do
  • Use monochrome backgrounds (black, white, gray)
  • Apply subtle industrial textures (brushed metal, concrete)
  • Use grayscale gradients with smooth transitions
  • Apply glass/blur effects over dark backgrounds
  • Use technical grid patterns at low opacity
  • Ensure minimum 4.5:1 contrast ratio for text
  • Test logo visibility on every background
  • Use dark backgrounds for hero and premium sections
  • Keep backgrounds simple so content stands out
  • Use the official color values exactly (#000, #1E1E20, #F5F5F7, #FFF)
Don't
  • Use colored backgrounds (blue, red, yellow, green, etc.)
  • Apply decorative or organic textures (watercolor, floral)
  • Use rainbow or multi-color gradients
  • Use stock photos as full-bleed backgrounds
  • Apply heavy, high-contrast patterns
  • Place logo on backgrounds with insufficient contrast
  • Use animated or moving backgrounds in static contexts
  • Mix warm and cool tones in backgrounds
  • Apply backgrounds that distract from content hierarchy
  • Use approximate or off-brand gray values

Background Approval Required

Any background style not explicitly covered in this guide requires written approval from the Koleex brand management team before use. When in doubt, default to solid black or white.
