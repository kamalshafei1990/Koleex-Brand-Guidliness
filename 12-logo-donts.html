Logo Do & Don't

A comprehensive visual reference for correct and incorrect logo usage, protecting the Koleex identity from misuse across all applications.

12.1 PURPOSE

Protect the Logo

This section exists to safeguard the Koleex logo from misuse and maintain a consistent, recognizable brand appearance across every touchpoint and medium.

Brand Protection

Consistency builds trust. Every deviation weakens the brand.

The logo is the single most recognizable element of the Koleex identity. When used incorrectly, it damages credibility and dilutes brand equity. These guidelines must be followed by every person and partner who handles the Koleex mark.

12.5 SPECIAL CASES

Production Exceptions

Certain physical production methods require slight adaptations. These are the only acceptable deviations from standard logo rules.

Metal Engraving
Fine details in the K and X letterforms may be slightly simplified for engraving on metal surfaces. Minimum stroke width must remain above 0.3mm. Always use vector-based artwork supplied by the brand team.
Laser Cutting
For laser-cut applications on wood, acrylic, or metal, the logo may use a single-weight outline version. Inner counters of the O and E letterforms must maintain a minimum 1mm width to avoid material breakage.
Embossing & Debossing
The logo may be embossed or debossed without color, relying on texture alone. Ensure sufficient depth (minimum 0.5mm) and use only the primary horizontal wordmark. The symbol mark is not approved for blind embossing below 15mm width.
Hot Foil Stamping
Silver or black foil stamping is approved. Gold foil is not permitted. Minimum logo width for foil stamping is 25mm to preserve letterform integrity. Always request a proof before full production.
Embroidery
A simplified embroidery-specific version is available from the brand asset library. Standard vector files must not be used for embroidery. Minimum embroidered logo width is 40mm. Use a maximum of 2 thread colors (black + white or silver).
Rubber Stamps & Ink Seals
The logo may be adapted for single-color rubber stamps. Fine details should be removed from the stamp master. Minimum stamp size is 30mm wide. Only black or silver ink is approved for official brand stamps.
12.6 SUMMARY

Do & Don't Summary

A quick-reference checklist covering all critical logo usage rules.

Do
  • Use official logo files from the brand asset library
  • Maintain minimum clear space around the logo
  • Use black or white versions on solid backgrounds
  • Scale proportionally from vector source files
  • Place logo on clean, uncluttered backgrounds
  • Respect minimum size requirements (25mm print, 80px digital)
  • Ensure high contrast between logo and background
  • Contact the brand team when unsure about usage
Don't
  • Stretch, compress, rotate, or skew the logo
  • Apply gradients, shadows, or effects to the logo
  • Use the logo in unapproved colors
  • Place the logo on busy or photographic backgrounds
  • Outline the logo or remove the fill
  • Add text, taglines, or decorations to the logo
  • Use the logo below minimum size requirements
  • Enclose the logo in shapes (circles, squares, etc.)

Logo Integrity Is Non-Negotiable

No Distortion No Effects No Recoloring Clear Space Always High Contrast Vector Only

When in doubt, refer to these guidelines or contact the Koleex brand team. Every application of the logo reflects the entire brand.
