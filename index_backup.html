01 — INTRODUCTION

Introduction

The foundation and purpose of the Koleex International Group brand identity system.

Purpose of This Document

This Brand Guidelines document defines the complete identity system of Koleex International Group.

It establishes the rules, standards, and principles that govern how the brand must appear, communicate, and be applied across all platforms and materials. This document ensures that the Koleex brand remains consistent, recognizable, and professional across all touchpoints.

Design Guide Brand System Identity Control Document
Objectives of the Brand Guidelines

The main objectives of this document are:

Maintain brand consistency across all platforms
Establish a clear visual identity system
Establish a clear communication and tone system
Provide design rules for all designers and teams
Ensure all brand materials look like they belong to the same company
Protect the brand from inconsistent usage
Support long-term brand growth and expansion
Create a recognizable global brand identity
Provide a reference for all internal and external partners
Standardize design, UI, packaging, print, and digital materials

02 — BRAND OVERVIEW

Brand Overview

Understanding what Koleex International Group is, how it is positioned, and the strategic direction of the brand.

About Koleex International Group

An international group operating across multiple business sectors.

Koleex International Group operates across manufacturing, international trade, technology, software, automation, smart systems, and future technologies. The group focuses on building technology-driven businesses, industrial products, digital systems, and global operations across different industries and markets.

Koleex is positioned as a group structure rather than a single product company. This allows the brand to operate in multiple sectors while maintaining one unified identity system. The brand must therefore be flexible enough to support different industries but consistent enough to always look like one organization.

The company operates with a long-term vision and focuses on building systems, products, technologies, and businesses that can grow and evolve over time rather than focusing only on short-term products.

Manufacturing International Trade Technology Software Automation Smart Systems Future Technologies

MOTION

Animation Principles

Motion should feel natural, purposeful, and refined. Every animation reinforces the premium Koleex experience.

Ease Out

Primary easing for entrances. Elements decelerate as they arrive at their final position. Duration: 300–500ms.

Spring

Interactive elements like buttons and cards. Slight overshoot creates a tactile, responsive feel. Duration: 400–600ms.

Bounce

Attention-drawing micro-interactions. Use sparingly for success states and celebratory moments. Duration: 600–800ms.

VOICE & TONE

How We Communicate

Our voice is confident, clear, and authoritative — reflecting our position as a global industry leader.

Authoritative

We speak with confidence earned from experience. Our words carry weight and credibility.

Direct

We value clarity over complexity. Every word serves a purpose — no filler, no jargon.

Global

Our language is inclusive and culturally aware, resonating across international markets.

Premium

Our tone reflects quality and sophistication, matching the caliber of our brand and offerings.

Do

  • Use active, confident language
  • Keep sentences short and impactful
  • Focus on value and outcomes
  • Be culturally sensitive and inclusive
  • Maintain a professional, premium tone

Don't

  • Use slang, buzzwords, or filler
  • Overcomplicate with industry jargon
  • Be vague or non-committal
  • Use humor that doesn't translate globally
  • Sound casual or unprofessional
