Brand Personality

Brand Personality describes the brand as if it were a person.

It defines how the brand behaves, communicates, appears, and presents itself to the world. The brand personality must be consistent across everything the company does.

Summary of Brand Personality

A global, professional, technology-driven, industrial, structured, minimal, and intelligent brand with an engineering mindset and a calm but confident presence.

The brand should always look: Engineered. Structured. Minimal. Professional. Global. Technological. Industrial. Intelligent.

Engineered Structured Minimal Professional Global Technological Industrial Intelligent

If something looks playful, colorful, messy, crowded, or decorative, it is not aligned with the Koleex brand personality.
