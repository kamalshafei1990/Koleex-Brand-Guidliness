Logo Usage Rules

Comprehensive rules governing how the Koleex logo must be used, placed, sized, and protected across every brand application and material.

Section 11.1

Purpose of Logo Usage Rules

Logo usage rules ensure consistent, clear, professional, and recognizable logo usage across all materials. These guidelines protect the integrity of the Koleex brand by defining exactly how the logo should appear in every context, ensuring every touchpoint reinforces brand recognition and trust.

Consistency Clarity Professionalism Recognition Brand Integrity

11.3 — Logo Clear Space

The minimum clear space around the logo equals the height of the letter "K" in the Koleex wordmark. This space must be maintained on all four sides of the logo. Never place text, images, or other elements within this zone.

K-height K-height K-height K-height
Minimum clear space = height of the letter "K". Must be kept on all 4 sides at all times.
11.4 — Minimum Logo Size

To ensure legibility and brand integrity, the Koleex logo must never be reproduced below the minimum size requirements. Use the icon version for smaller applications and the badge for very small placements.

11.5 — Logo Placement Rules

The logo should be placed in consistent, predictable positions across all materials. Follow grid alignment and use approved placement zones: top left, top center, bottom center, bottom left, bottom right, and center (for covers only).

Top left — primary position for documents, web, and UI
Top center — formal documents, certificates, invitations
Bottom center — packaging, print materials, presentations
Bottom left — secondary documents, footers
Bottom right — business cards, tags, labels
Center — covers, splash screens, hero sections only
Always align the logo with the layout grid, margins, columns, and surrounding content elements.
11.6 — Logo Color Usage

The logo must only appear in approved colors depending on the background and application context. Use black on light backgrounds, white on dark backgrounds, and gray when a muted appearance is required.

Black on White
White on Black
Gray on Neutral
Metallic finish — for machines and industrial equipment
Engraved — for metal surfaces and plaques
Embossed — for premium packaging and stationery
Monochrome — for print with color limitations
11.7 — Background Control

The logo must always appear on backgrounds that provide sufficient contrast and clarity. Approved backgrounds include solid colors, clean photography, and industrial textures. Avoid busy, complex, or low-contrast backgrounds.

Solid Black
Solid White
Neutral Gray
Busy Pattern
Color Gradient
Low Contrast
11.8 — Alignment Rules

The logo must always be aligned with the layout structure of the material. Consistent alignment reinforces visual order and professionalism across all brand touchpoints.

Align with page margins at all times
Align to grid columns in multi-column layouts
Align with text blocks and headings
Align with images and visual content
Follow the overall layout structure consistently
Never place the logo randomly or without grid reference
11.9 — Logo on Different Materials

The logo must be adapted correctly for each material and application type while maintaining brand consistency. Each context has specific size, position, and treatment requirements.

Document — Top Left, Small
Packaging — Front Center, Large
Website — Top Left Header
Software UI — Top Bar
Vehicle — Door Center
Exhibition — Large Wall
Documents — top left corner, small size, with clear space
Packaging — front center, large size for maximum visibility
Machines — metal badge or engraved on equipment surface
Website — top left in header, consistent across all pages
Software UI — top bar, sidebar, or splash screen
Vehicles — door center or side panel, scaled for visibility
Uniforms — left chest embroidery or back center print
Exhibition — large on walls, reception desk, digital screens

Follow these rules strictly to protect the integrity and consistency of the Koleex logo across all applications.

Do
  • Use original, unmodified logo files from official assets
  • Maintain the clear space zone on all four sides
  • Use only approved colors: black, white, or gray
  • Use SVG or high-resolution PNG for digital
  • Use vector formats (AI, EPS, PDF) for print
  • Place on clean, uncluttered backgrounds
  • Ensure high contrast between logo and background
  • Scale the logo proportionally at all times
  • Respect minimum size requirements
  • Align consistently across all brand materials
  • Follow placement rules for each material type
Don't
  • Stretch or compress the logo
  • Change proportions or aspect ratio
  • Change the logo colors to unapproved values
  • Add drop shadows to the logo
  • Apply gradients or color fills
  • Add outlines or stroke effects
  • Rotate the logo at any angle
  • Skew or distort the logo
  • Apply 3D, glow, or other effects
  • Place on busy or complex backgrounds
  • Place inside shapes or containers
  • Recreate or redraw the logo manually
  • Change the font or letter spacing
  • Add extra text near the logo
  • Combine with other company logos
  • Use distorted or low-resolution files
  • Use low-resolution screenshots as logo
  • Place too close to edges of materials
  • Place the logo randomly without grid reference
  • Use on backgrounds with similar colors
  • Use on patterned or gradient backgrounds

Mandatory Brand Compliance

Any use of the Koleex logo that deviates from these rules must be reviewed and approved by the brand management team before publication or production. Unauthorized modifications are strictly prohibited and may result in brand inconsistency.
