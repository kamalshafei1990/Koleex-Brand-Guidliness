Social Media Guidelines

Consistent visual identity, tone, layout, and brand image across all social media platforms.

23.1 Purpose

Social media guidelines ensure consistency across all platforms. Every post, story, video cover, and digital announcement must follow the Koleex brand identity to maintain a unified, professional presence.

23.2 Brand Principles for Social Media

All social media content must reflect the Koleex brand as modern, industrial, and professional. Every post should feel like it belongs to a technology company, not a generic brand.

Should Be
  • Clean layout with minimal design
  • Strong typography with clear hierarchy
  • Dark background preferred
  • High contrast between elements
  • Professional photography only
  • Industrial / technology feeling
  • Simple composition with clear message
  • Large margins and breathing room
  • Grid-based alignment
  • Consistent brand colors only
Should NOT Be
  • Cluttered or busy layouts
  • Too many colors in one post
  • Low-quality or pixelated images
  • Stretched or distorted elements
  • Inconsistent fonts or typography
  • Generic stock photography
  • Cartoon or illustrated graphics
  • Neon or bright random colors

23.4 Color System for Social Media

Social media posts primarily use a dark background with white text. Gray is used for secondary information. Accent colors are for highlights only. Gradients are allowed when following brand direction.

Avoid

Do NOT use bright random colors, neon colors, too many colors in one post, or cartoon-style color palettes. Keep it minimal, dark, and professional.

23.5 Typography

Use Helvetica Neue as the primary typeface. Bold for titles, regular for body. Headlines should be large and short. Text should follow consistent spacing and grid alignment.

Typography Hierarchy

Description Precision engineering solutions for modern manufacturing.

Regular weight for body text
Large headlines, short text
Consistent spacing and grid alignment
23.6 Photography Style

Photography should convey industrial precision and technology. Use dark backgrounds, dramatic lighting, high contrast, and minimal compositions. Focus on machines, mechanical details, and factory environments.

Industrial machines & factory
Technology details & close-ups
Mechanical parts & metal textures
Office & exhibition environments
Dark background, dramatic lighting
High contrast, minimal composition

Avoid

Stock smiling people, cartoon graphics, low-quality images, busy backgrounds, over-edited photos, or generic lifestyle imagery.

23.9 Logo Usage on Social Media

The logo must have a consistent position on every post. Use white logo on dark backgrounds, dark logo on light backgrounds. Maintain clear space around the logo. Never stretch, add effects, or rotate the logo.

Never make logo oversized
23.10 Do & Don't

Summary of all social media design rules to ensure consistency and quality across all platforms and post types.

Industrial
Innovation

Incorrect
Do
  • Use dark background (black or near-black)
  • Use white text for primary content
  • Keep layouts clean and minimal
  • Follow grid alignment for all elements
  • Use large margins and breathing room
  • Place logo consistently on every post
  • Use brand typography (Helvetica Neue)
  • Use professional industrial photography
  • Maintain clear hierarchy (label, title, desc)
  • Keep posts consistent across all platforms
  • Use templates for every post type
  • High contrast between elements
  • Simple composition with clear message
  • Technology and industrial feeling
Don't
  • Use bright or colorful backgrounds
  • Mix multiple fonts in one post
  • Create cluttered or busy layouts
  • Use neon or cartoon colors
  • Place logo randomly or centered oversized
  • Stretch, rotate, or add effects to logo
  • Use low-quality or pixelated images
  • Use generic stock photography
  • Use too many colors in one post
  • Ignore consistent spacing and margins
  • Use inconsistent typography
  • Add decorative or cartoon graphics
  • Use random layout without template
  • Over-edit or over-process images
