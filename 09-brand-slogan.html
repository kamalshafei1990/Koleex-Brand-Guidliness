Brand Slogan

The official slogans, their meaning, usage rules, and placement guidelines across all brand materials.

Brand Slogan

KOLEEX — Shaping the Future.

This is the official main slogan of Koleex International Group. It represents the long-term vision of the company as an international group involved in industry, technology, systems, and future development.

Future Technology Industry Systems Development Innovation Global Engineering

Meaning of the Slogan

"Shaping the Future" means:

Building future technologies
Developing new systems
Creating industrial solutions
Expanding globally
Investing in new industries
Designing future products
Developing software and automation
Building systems and platforms
Improving industry performance
Creating long-term impact
Engineering the future of industry and technology
Participating in global development
Creating new business opportunities
Supporting industrial evolution
Developing smart systems and automation
Designing modern industrial solutions
The slogan is not limited to machines or industrial products. It represents the entire group and its future direction.

Slogan Usage Rules

Slogan should follow these rules:

Do not change the wording
Do not add extra words
Do not change capitalization
Do not stretch the slogan
Do not rotate the slogan
Do not use different fonts
Do not change spacing randomly
Do not place slogan too close to logo
Do not use slogan in very small size
Do not use slogan everywhere
Use slogan mainly in corporate materials
Do not overuse the slogan in every design
Use slogan in presentations/website/company profile/exhibitions
Posters sometimes may include slogan
Packaging usually does not include slogan unless corporate packaging
Software UI should not include slogan
Machine body usually should not include slogan
Business cards may include slogan
Email signature may include slogan
Slogan should be used strategically, not everywhere.

Shaping the Future.

Option 3
Shaping the Future.

Logo top center, Slogan bottom center

Option 4
Shaping the Future.

Logo top left, Slogan bottom left

Option 5
Shaping the Future.

Logo center, Slogan below center

Spacing between logo and slogan must be consistent and not too close.
Slogan Typography

Slogan typography should be:

Should be:
  • Helvetica Neue
  • Regular or Light weight
  • Normal or slightly increased letter spacing
  • Smaller than logo text
  • Clean and minimal
  • Black or white color
  • Sometimes gray
  • Simple and clean
Should NOT be:
  • Not Bold
  • Not Italic
  • Not decorative
  • Not handwritten
  • Not condensed
  • Not extended
  • Should not use bright colors
  • Should not use gradient
  • Should not use shadow
  • Should not use outline
Slogan should look minimal and professional.
